Engagement Approach

How the work gets done

Three structured phases, each building on the previous. Progress is tracked with KPIs agreed at kick-off. Click each phase to expand.

01
Phase 1
Discover & Baseline
Weeks 1–3
▼
›Kick-off meeting with leadership and team leads to align on goals and success criteria
›Agile Maturity Assessment — structured interviews and observation of ceremonies
›Current-state process mapping — value stream, bottlenecks, handoff points
›Team health check — anonymous survey on psychological safety, clarity, and morale
›Stakeholder alignment workshop — agree on desired future state and measurable KPIs
›Delivery: Discovery Report with prioritised findings and proposed coaching roadmap
02
Phase 2
Coach & Transform
Weeks 4–10 (or per agreed timeline)
▼
›Embedded coaching: attend and facilitate Sprint Planning, Dailies, Retros, Reviews
›Scrum Master mentoring — 1-to-1 sessions to build internal capability that outlasts the engagement
›Product Owner coaching — backlog hygiene, user story quality, prioritisation frameworks
›Process workshops: Definition of Done, estimation techniques, capacity planning
›DevOps integration session — align CI/CD pipeline practices with sprint cadence
›Fortnightly leadership briefings — progress updates, blocker escalation, metric review
›Bi-weekly retrospective of the coaching engagement itself — we apply Agile to our own work
03
Phase 3
Sustain & Handover
Final 2–3 weeks
▼
›Capability transfer — Scrum Masters and team leads sustain without external coaching dependency
›Documentation package — playbooks, ceremony guides, metric dashboards
›Final Agile Maturity Assessment — measure progress against Phase 1 baseline
›Retrospective with leadership — lessons learned and next 6-month recommendations
›Optional: retained advisory support (2 hrs/month) for 3 months post-engagement at no extra charge
